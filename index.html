Ketel One Rebranding

Still heritage, just no longer stuck in grandpa’s liquor cabinet

Ketel One Rebranding

slogan and tagline

"Savour the Legacy, Embrace the Thrill"
"Elevate Your Nights, Not Your Budget"

A branding refresh that took Ketel One from dated to deliberate. The goal was to honor its roots while reshaping its presence for a newer, sleeker market — more intentional layout, more edge, less ornamental fluff. Think refined, not forgotten.

The goal? Make Ketel One feel less like your dad’s go-to and more like something you’d actually post about. The rebrand keeps the heritage but trades the stiffness for clarity, edge, and relevance — reshaping everything from packaging to digital presence so it speaks to a new generation without losing its roots.
